Brownie

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of chocolate powder
  • 1 cup of wheat flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 200 grams of butter
  • 4 eggs

Method of preparation

  1. In a bowl, place the eggs, sugar and beat with the help of a whisk or fork
  2. Then add the butter and chocolate powder
  3. Mix everything and then add the wheat flour
  4. Stir the dough until it is homogeneous
  5. Then, pour the dough into a mold greased with chocolate powder
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 35 minutes
  7. Remove from oven and serve
